# Image Setup Instructions

## Professional Photos Integration

To integrate your professional photos into the portfolio:

1. **Rename and save your photos as:**
   - `vishnu-professional-1.jpg` - For hero section (mountain/nature photo)
   - `vishnu-professional-2.jpg` - For about section (indoor portrait or waterfall photo)
   - `vishnu-professional-3.jpg` - For additional use (third photo)

2. **Place them in:** `public/` folder

3. **Recommended specifications:**
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - Size: Optimized for web (< 500KB each)
   - Dimensions: At least 600x600px for profile images
   - Quality: High resolution for professional appearance
